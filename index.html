About Mundus

Mundus is a centralized global intelligence dashboard designed to provide an exhaustive profile of any nation at the click of a button. Derived from the Latin word for "world", Mundus synthesizes complex geopolitical, demographic, and meteorological data into a singular, intuitive interface.

The application allows users to explore the intricate layers of a country, from its economic standing and government structure to its cultural demographics and real-time environmental conditions.

Key Features:
  • National Profiles: Comprehensive data on geography, official languages, currency, and government types.
  • Demographic Analytics: Visual breakdown of ethnic and religious compositions.
  • Economic Indicators: Real-time GDP per capita data to provide economic context.
  • Integrated Geospatial Mapping: Dynamic location of countries and capital cities.
  • Capital Intelligence: Real-time weather forecasts and environmental air quality monitoring for national capitals.
Libraries

Mundus leverages industry-standard open-source libraries to transform raw data into a visual experience:

  • - Powers the interactive geospatial engine, enabling precise mapping and capital city location services.
  • Chart.js
    - Utilized specifically for our cultural data visualizations, rendering responsive charts for ethnic groups and religious distributions.

Data Sources

Our data is aggregated from some of the most respected global databases and meteorological institutions:

  • RESTCountries API: The primary source for core national data including demographics, geography, and official state symbols.
  • The World Factbook: Provides the detailed cultural datasets used to generate our ethnic and religious distribution charts.
  • World Bank Open Data: Supplies our economic metrics, specifically the GDP per capita figures.
  • Open-Meteo: Delivers high-resolution weather and Air Quality Index (AQI) data for the world's capital cities.
